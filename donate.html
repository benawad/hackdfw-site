Become a Donor

Join us as a donor of HackDFW's annual hackathon! Known to be one of the largest collegiate and professional hackathons in the entire United States. As featured on NPR, WFAA, Dallas Morning News, and CW33, we look for support from our community to continue providing a stellar hackathon experience year after year. Recurring donors of HackDFW receive the following benefits:

  • First to know HackDFW news ($5 Level+)
  • Recognition on our website ($10 Level+)
  • Exclusive invitations to voice improvements to the HackDFW team ($25 Level+)
  • Exclusive invitations to our events ($50 Level+)

Looking to make a single one-time contribution? We extremely appreciate your help.
